# Hack Instruction Encoding

C-instruction format: 111a cccccc ddd jjj

For D=D+A:
- a = 0 (use A, not M)
- comp = 000010 (D+A)
- dest = 010 (D)
- jump = 000 (no jump)

Binary: 1110 0000 10 010 000 = 1110000010010000

For M=D:
- a = 0 (but we write to M)
- comp = 001100 (D)
- dest = 001 (M)
- jump = 000 (no jump)

Binary: 1110 0011 00 001 000 = 1110001100001000
